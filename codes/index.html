    Weight Management

    Weight management refers to the process of adopting long-term lifestyle modifications to maintain a healthy body weight based on a person's age, sex, and height.

    Birth Control guidence

    Birth control (or contraception) refers to methods or devices used to prevent pregnancy. Guidance involves helping individuals choose the most appropriate method based on health, lifestyle, and personal preferences.

    Hydration and Detox Programs

    These programs focus on maintaining proper fluid balance in the body and supporting the body’s natural detoxification processes (primarily through the liver, kidneys, and digestive system).

    Sleep Therapy

    Sleep therapy includes techniques and treatments used to improve sleep quality and manage disorders such as insomnia, sleep apnea, or restless leg syndrome

    Stress Management

    Stress management refers to a wide range of techniques and therapies aimed at controlling a person's level of stress, especially chronic stress, to improve everyday functioning

    Nutrition & Diet plans

    Nutrition refers to the intake of food in relation to the body’s dietary needs. A diet plan is a structured guide to eating that meets specific health or lifestyle goals (e.g., weight loss, managing diabetes, improving energy)
